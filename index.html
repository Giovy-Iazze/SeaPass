Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2025

1. Who We Are

SeaPass is an Android application developed and maintained by Giovanni Iazzetta. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at iazzettagiovanni01@gmail.com.

2. Data We Collect

SeaPass collects only the data you explicitly enter into the app:

  • Embarkation records (vessel name, dates, rank, flag state)
  • Certificate details (type, issue date, expiry date)

We do not collect any data automatically, we do not use analytics, and we do not track your behaviour.

3. Google Account & Drive

If you choose to enable cloud backup, SeaPass requests access to your Google Account for the sole purpose of reading and writing a backup file in your Google Drive App Data folder.

  • The App Data folder is a private, hidden folder that only SeaPass can access — it is invisible to you and to any other app in your Google Drive.
  • We only store a JSON backup of your in-app data (embarkations and certificates).
  • We never read, modify, or access any other files in your Google Drive.
  • We never sell, share, or transfer your Google account data to third parties.
  • You can revoke access at any time from your Google Account permissions page.

The specific Google OAuth scope used is drive.appdata, which provides the minimum access necessary to offer backup and restore functionality.

4. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using the Android Room database. Cloud backup is optional and stored exclusively in your personal Google Drive App Data folder. We do not operate any servers and do not store your data on our infrastructure.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear its data. To delete your cloud backup, revoke SeaPass's access from your Google Account permissions page; this will make the App Data folder inaccessible and the backup will be automatically removed.

7. Children's Privacy

SeaPass is intended for adult maritime professionals and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact: iazzettagiovanni01@gmail.com
